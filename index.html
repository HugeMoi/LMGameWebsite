Gaming is new. It has quite a while to go before it can boast of having a century's worth of media to sift through; like the film and music industry has. Yet, despite its infancy, I find it to be one of the most impressive forms of entertainment out there.
Gaming allows a level of interactivity and expression from the user that isn't possible in other forms of media. It's also one that manages to consistently provide people with accomplishment.
Games can be found anywhere. You can play them on your computer, mobile, your consoles. You can even play them in your browser, via HTML.

Pac-Man Fun Facts:
    The idea for the character Pacman came around when a designer took a slice out of his pizza.
    The first machine was installed in a movie theatre in Tokyo, on 22nd of May, 1980.
    It was originally called Puck Man, but the US distributor was worried that the word Puck being modified into something ruder.
